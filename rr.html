About the show

Rachael Ray's Kids Cook-Off is a new take on intense culinary competitions. The twist? Young genius chefs! All eight chegs enter the kitchen with a chance to win $20,000 towards their culinary education along with their own webseries the The Food Network site.

Each episode is hosted by Rachael Ray and a surprise co-host/ judge. The kids are tasked with a "short challenge", followed by the actual elimination round. Previous judges include: Robert Irvine, Daymon Patterson ("Daym Drops"), Sunny Anderson, Alex Guarnaschelli, Amanda Frietag, Marcus Samuelssan, and Scott Conant.
